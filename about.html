About GYB Model Science Secondary School

About GYB

The GYB Model Science Secondary School, Adankolo, Lokoja is an ultra-modern public institution established by the administration of former Governor Yahaya Bello. Originally functioning as the Government Day Secondary School (GDSS) Adankolo, it was completely rebuilt and transformed into a premier educational institution to promote an educational revolution in Kogi State.

Background and Development Transformation

The old Government Day Secondary School (GDSS) Adankolo was completely demolished and rebuilt from the ground up. The transformation produced an international-standard learning environment equipped with modern classrooms, laboratories, libraries and sporting facilities for students across Kogi State.

Inauguration

The newly constructed GYB Model Science Secondary School was officially commissioned in 2022 and has since become one of the leading public secondary schools in Kogi State.

Laboratories

The school possesses modern science laboratories equipped for practical learning in Biology, Chemistry, Physics and Computer Science, giving students hands-on scientific experience.

Technology (CBT Centre)

The school operates a modern Computer-Based Test (CBT) Centre capable of accommodating hundreds of students simultaneously. The facility prepares students for modern digital examinations such as JAMB CBT and other computer-based assessments.

Library

The modern library provides students with a quiet learning environment, a wide range of textbooks, reference materials and digital learning resources that support academic excellence and independent study.

Sports Facilities

The school encourages physical fitness through modern sporting facilities including football, basketball, volleyball and other recreational activities that promote teamwork and leadership.

Recent History and Continued Support

The succeeding administration under Governor Ahmed Usman Ododo has continued to support the institution through regular inspections, educational development projects and sustained free education policies aimed at improving learning outcomes across Kogi State.

Academic Excellence

GYB Model Science Secondary School has established itself as one of the leading public secondary schools in Kogi State. Students have represented the state in numerous academic competitions including debates, spelling bees, science competitions and other educational programmes, demonstrating excellence, discipline and innovation.
