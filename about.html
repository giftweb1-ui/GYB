About Our School

The GYB Model Science Secondary School, Adankolo, Lokoja is an ultra-modern public institution established by the administration of former Governor Yahaya Bello . Originally functioning as the Government Day Secondary School (GDSS) Adankolo, it was completely rebuilt and retrofitted into a premier educational facility to promote an educational revolution in Kogi State.Background and DevelopmentTransformation: The old, traditional structures of GDSS Adankolo were demolished and rebuilt from the ground up to provide world-class, international-standard facilities to the public .Inauguration: The modern complex was completed and officially commissioned as the GYB Model Science Secondary School in the latter half of 2022.Vision: The initiative was championed by the Yahaya Bello administration to bridge the gap between expensive private international schools and public schools, offering top-tier education to children from all socioeconomic backgrounds .Core Facilities and UpgradesThe facility is highly regarded as one of the best secondary education centers in Northern Nigeria due to its state-of-the-art infrastructure :Laboratories: Fully equipped, high-capacity science laboratories designed to rival those of major tertiary institutions.Technology: A massive Computer-Based Test (CBT) center capable of sitting hundreds of students simultaneously.Sports Complex: Professional-grade courts for football, basketball, volleyball, and lawn tennis to encourage extracurricular development.Recent History and LegacyContinued Support: The succeeding administration under Governor Ahmed Ododo has maintained strong support for the institution, conducting unscheduled visits to assess student welfare and continuing the state's free education policies.Academic Excellence: The school has cemented a reputation for producing highly skilled students, consistently representing the state and winning various academic competitions, including regional Spelling Bees .
